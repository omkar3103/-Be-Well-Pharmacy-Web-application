About Us

BE WELLPHARMACY

BE WELL PHARMACY Online is a comprehensive, free-to-access, online database containing information on drugs and drug targets. As both a bioinformatics and a cheminformatics resource, we combine detailed drug (i.e. chemical, pharmacological and pharmaceutical) data with comprehensive drug target (i.e.pathway) information. BE WELL PHARMACY is for medicinal chemists, pharmacists,physicians, students and the general public. Because of its broad scope, comprehensive referencing, and detailed data descriptions,enabling major advancements across the data-driven medicine industry..
